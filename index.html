Rock, Paper, Scissors

  • The game consists of 5 rounds.
  • Each player simultaneously selects one of three options: Rock, Paper, or Scissors.
  • Rock beats Scissors, Scissors beats Paper, and Paper beats Rock.
  • The winner of each round earns a point.
  • The player who wins the majority of the 5 rounds is declared the winner of the game.
  • If there is no clear winner after 5 rounds, the game ends in a draw.
